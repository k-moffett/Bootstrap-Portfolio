About Me

Hello, my name is Kurtis Moffett. I was born in San Diego and lived here until I was eleven, then I lived in upstate New York for 12 years. I went to college in upstate New York for Music Industry. At the time I was working for a catering company, a performing arts center, and for a furniture moving company. Additionally, I was playing gigs with my band whenever possible. I moved back to San Diego in 2014, and started working as a guitar assembler for Taylor Guitars on the nightshift in the final assembly department. I then obtained a job with CBS Radio under one of the digital media companies, Eventful Inc. I am currently looking to progress my career in the exciting field web development!
